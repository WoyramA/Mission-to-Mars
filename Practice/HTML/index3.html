There once lived an introverted young little girl from Accra. She had dreams of being a lawyer as she was very argumentative as a child. Life happened and three decades down the line, she found herself as a housewife after obtaining two degrees and equiping herself with almost a decade worth of specialized healthcare skills. This story delves into her experiences and numerous happenings that have led to her current situation.

There goes a saying that "If you do not tell your story, it will be told for you and not in the way you wish it to be told"
